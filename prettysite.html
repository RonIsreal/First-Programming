The City

Tel Aviv, the second most populous city in Israel, after Jerusalem, is a city full of life and activities. With people from all over the world, the city is known to be open to all kinds of cultures and customs. With a population of approximately 450,000 people, the city has a lot to offer, from exquisite restaurants to nonstop nightlife. Check below a couple tips for enjoying the city and do some sightseeing.

The Beaches

The Beaches

With 14 kilometers of shore line divided between sixteen beaches - all facing the Mediterranean Sea -, Tel Aviv has lots of sunbathing places to offer. The beaches have all unique aspects, since pet friendly beaches up to sport parlours! In Tel Aviv, you are sure to find a place to tan and hang out!
